Welcome to your Vehicle Insurance Policy Guide! Here are the rules regarding your coverage with us:

Eligibility & Plans
- We insure Private and Commercial vehicles operating on Petrol, Diesel, Hybrid, EV, or CNG.
- Electric Vehicle (EV) specific plans are exclusively available to EV fuel-type vehicles.
- Vehicles over 15 years old are not eligible for new insurance coverage.
- You can buy coverage for durations between 1 and 5 years. Choosing a longer duration earns you a Commitment Discount, which goes up to 12% for a 5-year plan.
- Returning customers who renew their policies receive an additional 5% Loyalty Discount.

Payments & Renewals
- Your policy officially becomes active once your first premium is paid.
- Subsequent premium payments are strictly due within a 30-day grace period. If you fail to pay within 30 days of the due date, your policy will be cancelled automatically.
- Renewals can secure continuous coverage if completed within 30 days before or 30 days after your current policy ends. Please note that policies with outstanding balances cannot be renewed.

Transfers
- If you sell your vehicle, you can seamlessly transfer your active policy to the new owner for a flat fee of ₹500.
- You cannot initiate a policy transfer if you have an active, pending claim on the policy.
