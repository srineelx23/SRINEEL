Policy & Coverage Protocols

Policy Coverage & Exclusions

VIMS provides financial protection against physical damage or bodily injury resulting from traffic collisions and other mechanical incidents.

  • Inclusions: Own damage, third-party liability, fire, theft, explosion, and natural calamities.
  • Exclusions: Wear and tear, electrical breakdown (without add-on), aging of parts, driving under influence, and unauthorized vehicle modification.
  • Supported Claim Types: Damage Claims (OD), Third Party Liability (TPL), Theft Recovery, and Constructive Total Loss (CTL).

Premium Computation Model

The base premium is derived using an actuarial formula based on vehicle risk and category.

Vehicle Category: Rates vary for Car, Two-Wheeler, and Heavy Commercial vehicles.
Age Factor (MFY): Older vehicles attract higher premiums due to maintenance risk.
IDV Basis: Value of premium is proportional to the Insured Declared Value.
Loading: Includes taxes, administrative fees, and roadside assistance charges.

Insured Declared Value (IDV) Calculation

IDV represents the maximum sum insured, which is fixed at the commencement of each policy term.

IDV = (Manufacturer's selling price - Depreciation as per age) + Add-ons

IDV directly correlates to the premium amount and is the cap for all claim payouts.

Special Clauses & Regulatory Conditions

  • Third-Party Liability: Settlement is strictly governed by statutory limits. Mandatory depreciation applies to all TP payouts (Zero-Dep add-on is NOT applicable).
  • Regulatory Constraint: Policies must be renewed within 30 days of expiry to avoid a new physical vehicle inspection.

Claim Lifecycle & Settlement

Eligibility Criteria

  • Claims must be filed while the policy is in Active status.
  • Evidence of damage (Images/Video) and a valid Repair Invoice (PDF) are mandatory.
  • Incident reporting must occur within 48 hours to avoid high-risk scrutiny.

Compulsory & Voluntary Deductibles

The deductible is the amount the policyholder pays out-of-pocket before the claim coverage begins.

  • Standard Deductible: Mandatory fixed amount (e.g. ₹500 or ₹1,000) per claim.
  • Payout Reduction: The deductible is subtracted from the "Repair Cost minus Depreciation" to compute the net payout.

Settlement Formula

Final Payout = (Approved Repair Cost - Depreciated Amount) - Mandatory Deductibles

Schedule 8: Total Loss Protocol (CTL)

The 75% Rule

A vehicle is deemed a Total Loss if the approved estimate of repair exceeds 75% of the vehicle's current IDV.

Payout: In Total Loss cases, the full IDV is paid out minus the deductible, and the vehicle title is transferred to VIMS.

Workflow Stages

Approved: Settlement finalized.

Governance & Intelligent Systems

Anti-Fraud Monitoring (AFM)

  • Temporal Flag: Claims on policies less than 48 hours old.
  • Asset Mismatch: PDF Registration Number must match Database ID.
  • Timing Risk: Multiple claims for similar incidents in 90-day cycles.

AI & OCR Analysis (VIMS-Bot)

We use PdfPig for text extraction and Groq for forensic summarization.

  • Extraction: Automatic identification of MFY, Invoice Amounts, and Incidents.
  • Limitations: Hand-written notes or blurred receipts may fail. Manual override is always available to the Officer.

Officer Decision Framework

All AI-processed claims are cross-reviewed by a certified Claims Officer.

  • Auto-Rejection: Triggers if Risk Score > 80% or vehicle class mismatch.
  • Manual Approval: Necessary for any payout exceeding ₹50,000.

System Limitations & Disclaimer

VIMS provides AI-assisted estimates. The final decision remains with the legal authority of the company. AI accuracy is estimated at 94% on clear digital documents.

Process & Compliance

Documentation Standards

Accepted: Clear PDF, High-res JPG/PNG.
Invalid: Blurred photos, watermarked scans, cropped edges.

Dispute & Re-evaluation

Policyholders may dispute a rejection within 15 days by submitting additional photographic evidence or a secondary repair estimate from an authorized brand garage.

Anomalous Scenarios

  • Incomplete Docs: Claim is moved to "Under Review" for 7 days pending updates.
  • Partial Approvals: Approved for parts but not labor (e.g., if unauthorized labor).
